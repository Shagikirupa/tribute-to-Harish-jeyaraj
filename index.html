Tribute to Harish jeyaraj

Harish Jeyaraj is a well-known Indian music composer and singer who primarily works in the Tamil film industry.
He has composed music for a number of successful films and is known for his versatilite
and innovation in his compositions.

Some of Harish Jeyaraj's popular compositions include "Unakaga" from the film "Bigil," "Vizhigalil Oru Vaanavil"
from the film "Deiva Thirumagal," and "Oh Penne" from the film "Vanakkam Chennai. " He has also won several
awards for his music, including the Filmfare Award for Best Music Director - Tamil for the
film "Unnaipol Oruvan."

In addition to his work in the film industry, Harish Jeyaraj has also composed music for television
shows, albums, and stage productions. He is highly regarded by both audiences and critics alike for his musical talent
and contributions to the Tamil music industry.
